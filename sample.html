Welcome to Our Dashboard

This dashboard allows you to visualize sales data, analyze trends, and make data-driven decisions. Here’s how to get started:

Navigating the Dashboard

  • Top Menu: Access different segments and data visualizations.
  • Line Chart: View sales trends over time. Use the indicators to add SMA, EMA, and MACD lines.
  • Project Rows: Review key metrics for different companies, including sales and percentage changes.

Using the Chart Indicators

Click on the ☰ button next to the chart to select different indicators like SMA (Simple Moving Average) or EMA (Exponential Moving Average). This helps in analyzing the sales trends more deeply.

Understanding the Data

  • Sales: Total sales value.
  • Pct Change: The percentage change in sales compared to the previous period.
  • Status: Indicates the performance health of each project or company.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

How do I download the data?
Click on the 'Download' button located at the top right corner of each chart to download the data in various formats.
Can I filter the data by date range?
Yes, use the slider below the chart to adjust the date range and view data for specific periods.
